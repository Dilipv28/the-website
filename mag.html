The Future of Minimalist Architecture in Urban Spaces

In the heart of the modern city, a new movement is taking root. Architects are moving away from the glass-and-steel giants of the past toward organic shapes and sustainable materials. This shift represents more than just a style change; it's a fundamental rethink of how we interact with our environment. By focusing on light, air, and natural flow, these designers are creating spaces that breathe.
